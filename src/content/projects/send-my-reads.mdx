Ever tried to send an epub to your kindle and got hit with the 30mb email attachement limit?

Introducing Send My Reads, we automatically reduce the size of your epub and send it directly into your kindle device.

It is a community powered and open-source project. [![support button of obsidian bible reference](https://img.shields.io/static/v1?label=Sponsor&message=%E2%9D%A4&logo=GitHub&color=%23fe8e86)](https://github.com/SitaGomes/send-my-reads)
